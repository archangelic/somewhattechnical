Starting my Journey

October 17 2016, 6:58 PM

I contacted my doctor a week ago to finally discuss starting HRT. I am scared, so scared and a little bit happy. I just want to know if it will make my body feel the way it should.

I am latching on to these hopes after feeling very dyphoric all weekend. Nothing about my body feels right. Event my beard, which I love dearly with all my heart, is making me feel not right.

I just felt like updating everyone. I feel like a baby trans just takkng my first step, and want everyone to see it.
